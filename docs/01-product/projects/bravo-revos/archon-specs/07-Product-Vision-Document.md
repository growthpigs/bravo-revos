# Product Vision Document - RevOS

## Executive Summary

**RevOS** is an AI-powered LinkedIn growth engine that transforms how coaches and small businesses generate qualified leads. By automating comment scraping, lead enrichment, and multi-channel outreach while maintaining authentic personalization, RevOS delivers what clients actually want: **jobs done, not dashboards to manage**.

**Market**: B2B coaches, consultants, and small agencies ($50B+ market)  
**Business Model**: SaaS ($2,000-5,000/client/month)  
**Competitive Edge**: AgentKit-powered automation that learns client voice and handles 90% of work via chat  
**Go-to-Market**: Agency fulfillment model - we run campaigns for clients

   - Care about ROI, not metrics

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: LinkedIn Changes ToS / Bans Automation

**Likelihood:** Medium  
**Impact:** Critical

**Mitigation:**
- Use Unipile (legal compliance layer)
- Conservative rate limits (50 DMs/hour, not 100)
- Human-in-loop for content (not fully automated)
- Diversify channels (email, SMS) so not 100% LinkedIn-dependent

### Risk 2: AI-Generated Content Feels Generic

**Likelihood:** Medium  
**Impact:** High (affects reply rates)

**Mitigation:**
- Cartridge system (learns client voice)
- Human approval for all content initially
- Mem0 learning loop (gets better over time)
- Fall back to templates if AI quality drops

### Risk 3: Competitors Copy Our Model

**Likelihood:** High (if we succeed)  
**Impact:** Medium

**Mitigation:**
- Build moat via cartridge learning data
- First-mover advantage (lock in best clients)
- Agency expertise hard to replicate
- Continuous innovation (V2 features)

### Risk 4: Unit Economics Don't Scale

**Likelihood:** Low  
**Impact:** Critical

**Mitigation:**
- Monitor API costs obsessively
- Cache Apollo results (reduce enrichment calls)
- Negotiate volume pricing with Unipile, OpenAI
- Raise prices if needed (90%+ margins = room)

### Risk 5: Can't Hire Fast Enough to Support Growth

**Likelihood:** Medium  
**Impact:** High

**Mitigation:**
- AgentKit handles 90% of support ("How do I...?")
- Self-service docs + video tutorials
- Hire support team in Month 6 (before scaling ads)
- Use RevOS itself to recruit (dogfooding)
