# RevOS Pricing & Business Model

## Pricing Tiers

### Tier 1: Starter - $2,000/month
- 1 LinkedIn account
- 100 leads/month scraped
- 500 Apollo credits/month
- 200 DMs/month
- Email sequences (unlimited)
- 1 cartridge
- Email support

### Tier 2: Growth - $3,500/month
- 1 LinkedIn account
- 300 leads/month scraped
- 1,000 Apollo credits/month
- 500 DMs/month
- Email + SMS sequences
- 3 cartridges
- Slack support
- Monthly strategy call

### Tier 3: Enterprise - $5,000/month
- 3 LinkedIn accounts (founder + team)
- Unlimited leads scraped
- 2,000 Apollo credits/month
- 1,000 DMs/month
- Full multi-channel (DM, email, SMS)
- Unlimited cartridges
- Dedicated Slack channel
- Weekly strategy calls
- Priority support

- License RevOS to marketing agencies

## Unit Economics

### Per Client (Growth Tier - $3,500/month)

**Revenue:** $3,500/month

**Direct Costs:**
| Service | Monthly Cost |
|---------|--------------|
| OpenAI API | $100 |
| Unipile (1 account) | $5 |
| Apollo.io (1,000 credits) | $100 |
| Instantly | $30 |
| Supabase | $25 |
| Mem0 | $20 |
| SMS (optional) | $50 |
| **Total** | **$330/month** |

**Gross Margin:** $3,170 (90.6%)

**Operating Costs (per client):**
| Item | Monthly Cost |
|------|--------------|
| Support (10 hrs @ $50/hr) | $500 |
| Infrastructure | $50 |
| **Total** | **$550/month** |

**Net Margin:** $2,620 (74.9%)
